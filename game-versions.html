Video Game Versioning

By Howard Jones, @anotherhowie

A silly package to transform semver versions into much more exciting video game versions. The result of a twitter conversation with @pobocks (https://twitter.com/pobocks/status/1052967711456813056)

The versioning is a mish-mash of Street Fighter, classic 80s arcade and 90s cash cow milking.

Also available as an npm package, because why not. (github)
